Frequently Asked Questions

What costs are associated with having a website?

Every website requires hosting and a domain name. Hosting pricing can very depending on where you host your website. Your domain name price can also vary depending on how common it is. The more common, the more expensive. Every client we work with gets an Onboarding Guide that walks you through each of these steps once we get started.

How long does it take to rank my website on Google?

The process of getting ranked in Google search results varies. However, typically clients see results within 6 months to 1 year. Our minimum agreement term for our SEO services is 6 months for this reason.

Can you create a website that I can host on a different platform?

Yes, I can create your website and export the code to you for hosting on a different platform. I do not offer the migration service itself, however I am willing to export you the code if that's what we agree on before beginning your project.
